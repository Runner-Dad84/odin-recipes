Creamy Garlic Pasta

Recipe Description

This dairy free pasta is a vegan alternative to fettucine alfredo.

Ingrediants

  • Olive oil - 1/2 tbs
  • Garlic minced - 4 gloves
  • Shallot finely chopped - 1 shallot
  • Vegetable broth - 2 cups
  • Unsweetened non-dairy milk - 1 1/2 cups
  • Salt - 1 tsp.
  • Dry fettuccine - 8 oz
  • Black pepper, dried oregano, red pepper flakes - to taste
  • Fresh basil chopped (optional garnish)

Instructions

  1. Heat olive oil in large skillet on medium high
  2. Add garlic, shallot and salt, stir. Cook until garlic and shallot are soft but no browned (about 2 minutes).
  3. Add vegetable broth, milk, salt and stir. Next add the pasta. Gentrly prod to ake sure all pasta is submerged in the liquid
  4. Bring to a boil, the reduce to a simmer. Gentrly stir to keep pasta from sticking. Cook for 20 minutes or until sauce has desired thickness.
