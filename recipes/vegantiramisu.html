Vegan Tiramisu

Recipe Description

This vegan tiramisu recipe is quick and easy to follow and not overly complicated.

Ingrediants

  • Silken tofu - 10.5 oz
  • Coconut cream - 1/2 cup
  • Sugar - 4tbsp
  • Plain biscuits - 7 oz
  • Unsweetened non-dairy milk - 1 1/2 cups
  • Stong coffee/ expresso - 1 cup
  • Dry fettuccine - 8 oz
  • Cocoa powder - optional
  • Dark chocolate - optional

Instructions

Preperation

  1. Refrigerate the coconut milk over night for better separation of the creamy and liquid parts.
  2. Brew the coffee

For the Creamy Layer

  1. Briefly drain the silken tofu, and put it in a blender bowl
  2. Scoop off the creamy, fatty part from the top of a can of coconut milk (about 150g per can). Add it (the creamy part) to the blender
  3. Add the sugar and blend until completely smooth
  4. Bring to a boil, the reduce to a simmer. Gentrly

Assembly

  1. Cover the bottom of your tiramisu dish in biscuits soaked in coffee.
  2. Cover the biscuits with a layer of the creamy mixture
  3. Add more layers of biscuits and cream, finishing with a layer of cream. How many layers you end up with depends on the size dish.
  4. sing a fine sieve, sprinkle on cocoa powder, and grate some dark chocolate on top.
  5. Refrigerate to let flavours blend and the tiramisu stiffen up. For best results, chill for at least 8 hours.
